MUNICIPAL & LAND USE PLANNING

Our municipal and land use planning lawyers advise municipalities, utilities, local boards, landowners and developers throughout Eastern Ontario.

Our lawyers represent clients on matters before Ontario's civil courts, the Ontario Municipal Board, lower and upper tier councils, land division committees and committees of adjustment.

Our work for municipal corporations includes advice in respect of accountability and transparency, access to information, meeting procedure, tax enforcement, by-law prosecution, Building Code Act and enforcement, by-law drafting and the drafting and negotiation of service agreements, joint service agreements and agreements with agencies of the provincial government.
