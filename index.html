Practice Areas

We focus on being trusted legal advisors for our clients. By listening to you and providing personalized attention we will develop a plan to best address your legal needs. Our lawyers will work with you and your other professional advisors to ensure you receive effective legal advice.

Corporate & Commercial

Our Corporate & Commercial practice focuses on the needs of small to medium sized businesses in Ottawa and Eastern Ontario.

Employment Law

We offer assistance with employment-related issues for both employees and employers.

Estate & Succession Planning

We provide a range of estate planning advice from simple wills and powers of attorney for individuals to more complicated estate plans for business owners.

Litigation

We represent both business clients and individuals when disputes arise.

Municipal & Land Use Planning

We assist a number of municipalities with various legal concerns.

Real Estate & Development

Our real estate team represents businesses and individuals in real estate transactions.

Personal Injury

We offer assistance with motor-vehicle accidents, SABS, slip-and-falls, and we can help you with your disability (LTD) claims and CPP-Disability claims.
