FOSTER v. FIRST CANADA STUDENT ULC o/a FIRST STUDENT
Court File No. 17-73084

SKS LAW LLP, on behalf of class representative Elaine Foster and members of the proposed class of plaintiffs, has commenced an action under the Class Proceedings Act in the Ontario Superior Court of Justice against First Canada ULC, which is a school bus transportation provider that operates under the business name First Student. The action was commenced in Ottawa on June 23, 2017 and has not yet been certified by the Court.

The representative plaintiff and proposed class of plaintiffs are claiming damages at common law for wrongful dismissal flowing from their unlawful mass termination of employment by First Student on or after June 25th, 2015.

The proposed class of plaintiffs include all persons who were non-unionized employees of First Student and whose employment in Ontario was terminated on June 25th , 2015 or shortly thereafter, excluding the directors and officers of First Canada ULC o/a First Student, and who were employed by First Student at its following Ottawa area branches:

  1. Nepean, located at 1027 Moodie Drive, K2R 1H4;
  2. West Carleton, located at 100 Cardevco Road, K0A 1L0;
  3. Cumberland, with operations at:
    1. 2015 Bantree Street, K1B 4X3;
    2. 1830 Trim Road, K4A 3P8; and
    3. 18 Jamie Street, K2E 6T6.
